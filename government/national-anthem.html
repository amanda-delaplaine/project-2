National Anthem

Iceland's national anthem was originally written and performed in 1874.
In 1944, Iceland adopted what was originally known as "O, Gud vors lands," as its national anthem.

Name: "Lofsongur" (Song of Praise)

Lyrics/Music: Matthias Jochumsson and Sveinbjorn Sveinbjornsson
